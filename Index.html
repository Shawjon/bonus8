Eggs, don't you just love them?

What is an egg?

An oval or round object laid by a female bird containing a developing embryo. The eggs are enclosed in a chalky shell

Why Eggs are great

  • They can be enjoyed anytime of day
  • They are very nutritious
  • They can improve your cholesterol

    • Egg Tips
